Raspberry Jam

Recipe

Raspberry Jam is one of my favorites, and thankfully it is easy to make! Follow these steps and you'll be able to create a yummy jam!

Ingredients

  • 5 handfuls of Raspberries
  • Pan filled with 5 cups water
  • 2 cups of sugar

Steps

  1. Add 2 cups of sugar to boiling water
  2. Throw 4 handfuls of Raspberries
  3. Stir until it solidifies
